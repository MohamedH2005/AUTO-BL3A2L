Car Maintenance Guide

Change your oil every 5,000 to 7,500 miles or every 6 months, whichever comes first. Use the recommended oil grade for your vehicle.
Rotate tires every 5,000 to 7,500 miles to ensure even wear. Check wheel alignment annually or if you notice uneven tire wear.
Have your brakes inspected at least once a year. Replace brake pads when they wear down to 3mm or if you hear squeaking noises.
Check battery terminals for corrosion. Most car batteries last 3-5 years. Test your battery annually, especially before winter.
Replace engine air filter every 15,000 to 30,000 miles. Cabin air filter should be replaced every 12,000 to 15,000 miles.
Flush and replace coolant every 30,000 miles or every 2-3 years. Check coolant level regularly and top up if needed.
